## What Is a [Bitcoin Mixer](https://btcmix.net/)?

A crypto mixer (or tumbler) is a service that breaks the traceable link between your sending and receiving wallets. It works by pooling your funds with those of other users and sending them out to fresh addresses, making blockchain analysis virtually impossible.

## Why Do You Need One?

- **Defense against chain analysis** — blockchain surveillance companies are hired by exchanges and governments to profile your transactions
- **Safety for large transfers** — moving significant amounts on a public ledger paints a target on your back
- **Financial privacy** — your spending habits and holdings should not be public knowledge
- **Protection from targeted phishing** — if attackers can see your wallet balance, they can craft personalized attacks against you
